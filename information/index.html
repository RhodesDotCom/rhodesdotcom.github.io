There is no parking at Oxford Town Hall.
Oxpens car park is open 24/7 and is about a 15 minute walk from the town hall.
Redbridge Park and Ride
From Oxford Town Hall we have a private bus to the venue.
There is very limited parking at Worton Kitchen Gardens, if you intend to drive there please let us know in advance.

Colour is encouraged!
